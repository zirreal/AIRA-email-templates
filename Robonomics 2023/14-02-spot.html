Share love with robots!
At Robonomics, we are convinced that in the nearest future complex robotics will be an inevitable part of our everyday lives, from basic manufacturing deployments that happen for years to latest adoption in logistics, hospitality and medical industries. Think of blockchain as a technology that enables you to remotely access and control a physical robot from anywhere in the world in real-time.
Merklebot team used Robonomics Network to enable remote access and control to a Boston Dynamics Spot robodog located in a laboratory in California. Look at the demo where you can draw anything and Spot will repeat your drawing to create a memorable NFT about your interaction. You can access the robot using your Polkadot account. Simply draw on the browser, send the transaction, and the robot will replicate your movements. As a result you will receive NFT with hand-drawn background minted on Singular marketplace.
